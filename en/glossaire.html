Glossary

Tecpro: Protective barriers used on F1 circuits, made from modular density foam reinforced with a double metal sheet. It has a very high absorption capacity.

GPDA: The GPDA is the Formula 1 Grand Prix drivers' association. It has been in existence since 1961 and has an advisory role in matters relating to Formula 1 safety.

BAFTA: The British Academy of Film and Television Awards are the British equivalent of the Oscars in American cinema and the Césars in French cinema.

Grammy Award: The Grammy Awards are presented every year since 1958 in the United States by the National Academy of Recording Art and Science to honour the best artists and technicians in the field of music.
