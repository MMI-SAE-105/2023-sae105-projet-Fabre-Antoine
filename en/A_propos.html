About us

If you are on this page, it means that you are interested in this site.
The website is a 1st semester BUT MMi project at the Université Nord Franche-Comté. The assignment was to create a mobile-first website in HTML CSS based on a film replica that we could each choose from. I chose the film Rush because it's one of my favourite films and because I'm a Formula 1 fan. I chose this replica because it represents the film and the characters well.
